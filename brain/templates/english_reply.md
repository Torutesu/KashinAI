# Template: English Reply

## Purpose

A template for translating or drafting customer-facing replies in English, for cases where a Japanese customer conversation needs to be shared with an English-speaking stakeholder, or for international prospects.

## Template Body

```txt
Hi {{recipient_name}},

Thank you for your message regarding {{topic_summary}}.

{{Response / decision, stated clearly and concisely}}

Next, we would like to confirm {{next step or question}}.

Please let us know if you have any questions in the meantime.

Best regards,
{{sender_name}} / Select Inc.
```

## Guidelines

- Keep sentences short and direct; avoid overly formal Japanese-style indirectness when translating
- State the conclusion first, then supporting detail
- Do not translate internal/confidential notes into the customer-facing draft
- When translating a Japanese thread, preserve the original meaning rather than a literal word-for-word translation

## Example (Project X status update, English version)

```txt
Hi Kitahara-san,

Thank you for your time in yesterday's requirements meeting.

We've agreed to scope the PoC data to exclude personally identifiable information, using only case-level attributes (industry, job category, etc.) instead.

Next, we'd like to share a cost-benefit estimate at our next meeting, along with a draft of the PoC agreement.

Please let us know if anything needs adjustment before then.

Best regards,
Select Inc.
```
